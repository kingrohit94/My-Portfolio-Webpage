Economy of India

Some facts about Indian economy:

The economy of India has transitioned from a mixed planned economy to a mixed middle-income developing social market economy with notable public sector in strategic sectors. It is the world's fifth-largest economy by nominal GDP and the third-largest by purchasing power parity (PPP).

About Indian Economy:

  • GDP Rank: 3rd
  • GDP Nominal: 4.1 trillion USD
  • GDP PPP: 14 trillion USD

Top 3 facts about India:

  1. Nearly 70% of India's GDP is driven by domestic consumption.
  2. The country remains the world's sixth-largest consumer market.
  3. With 476 million workers, the Indian labour force is the world's second-largest.
